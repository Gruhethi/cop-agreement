The COP 24 Agreement
Governance:
1. Compliance Committee:
Outlines the establishment of a Compliance Committee tasked with addressing issues related to non-compliance with the provisions of the Paris Agreement, ensuring governance transparency and accountability.
2. Paris Agreement Work Programme (PAWP):
 Encompasses a set of rules and procedures to operationalize the various provisions of the Paris Agreement, providing governance structure and processes for effective implementation.
3. Global Stocktake (GST):
Establishes a process for the periodic assessment of collective progress, informing Parties in updating and enhancing their actions and support, fostering governance dialogue.
4. Transparency Framework:
Enhances transparency in countries' actions by establishing a common framework, relying on a robust transparency and accounting system to provide clarity on action and support.
