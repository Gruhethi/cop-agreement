The COP 21 Agreement
Social:
1. Adaptation (Art. 7):
Aims to significantly strengthen national adaptation efforts, with a focus on support and international cooperation, addressing social vulnerabilities.
2. Loss and damage (Art. 8):
 Addresses the adverse effects of climate change, including extreme weather events, and emphasizes the role of sustainable development in reducing social risks.
3. Finance, technology, and capacity-building support (Art. 9, 10, and 11):
Reaffirms the obligations of developed countries to support developing countries in building clean, climate-resilient futures, emphasizing social well-being.
4. Climate change education, training, public awareness, public participation, and public access to information (Art 12):
Highlights the importance of enhancing education, training, public awareness, and participation, fostering social engagement and empowerment.
