The COP 24 Agreement
Social:
1. Adaptation Communication:
Provides guidance on the information countries should include in their Adaptation Communications, emphasizing adaptation priorities, actions, needs, and support received.
2. Finance:
 Addresses the provision of financial resources to support developing countries in their climate actions, enhancing transparency and tracking progress toward financial commitments for social well-being.
3. Capacity Building:
Provides guidance on capacity-building efforts to assist developing countries in implementing climate actions effectively, supporting institutional and individual capacity building for social development.
